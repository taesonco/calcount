# First, The why. 
Obesity in America is a crisis. Plain and Simple.  
We should never be so selfish and self-driven that we prioritize money over the lives of real people. When apps like myfitnesspal or LoseIt or CronoMeter take something that actually has real potential to change the lives of many with dieting and more importantly **educating** these people on what they consume, what it does to them, etc. And even moreso when they utilize data from the community, and then try to turn a profit from that data collected in good faith, it just puts a sour taste in my mouth. That being said, apps that are entirely open source don't really have a low bar of entry to understanding or tracking (like OpenFoodFacts is clunky, the data is spotty, and even more they don't have a clear and concise way to track or diet).
